Hello, I am in the role of designer and developer of a website for a real estate agency. They are offering residential properties - appartaments and houses, as well building plots. They are operating in Sofia, Bulgaria.

I want to build something different compared to the other agencies, where when you enter (my) feeling is that you see only - "who we are", "which appartaments are available" and basic stuff. And also they are all the same. See the examples below.

Examples:
- https://elitebuild.bg/
- https://balkanikahomes.eu
- https://bld.bg/bg

At the end all real estate agencies are offering the same thing, but my goal with this website is that when a new customer enters it, he or she has to see something different, something more than an appartaments or building plots. The first impression is important to me and I imagine the following concept:
At the beginning - a question "what are you looking for?". Then there are three choices - appartaments/house, building plot, just watching. Choice appartament and building plot are forwarding to a mini presentation and the third choice is lead to the main website.

The presentation for the appartament and houses contains of:
slide 1 - people sitting around a table discussing what the client needs and on the table are different peaces of paper and a laptop showing properties
slide 2 - 2 people one of the agency with a costume and the other one wearing casual clothes walking through a properties looking around.
slide 3 - papers for the purchase are organized from the agent
slide 4 - client recieves the key
slide 5 - if you need that call us

The presentation for the building plot:
slide 1 - people sitting around a table discussing what the client needs and on the table are different peaces of paper and a laptop showing maps and blueprints
slide 2 - 2 people one of the agency with a costume and the other one wearing casual clothes are next to a car, and in front of them is a green lawn. The camera is behind them, the agent is describing that this green lawn has everything for the idea of the client.
slide 3 - the agent is parking the car in front of the authorities to enter for processing the papers of the client
slide 4 - agency is organizing the building process, the agent is walking with the client through the construction site
slide 5 - celebrating the success of the ready building
slide 6 - the agents of the company are offering the build appartament/houses of the client to a clients, they are selling them
slide 7 - "if you need that call us"
